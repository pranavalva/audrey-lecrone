## Brand direction

- Warm, editorial, premium—not corporate stock or overly playful
- Soft natural light, cream/warm tones that harmonize with site palette (#F5F1EA, #D4534A, #D4A054)
- Photography-forward; avoid hard crop boxes on portraits (use soft edges)
